# MONETIZATION.md

**Owner:** Monetization Designer · **Canonical:** [Bible §14](./GAME-DESIGN-BIBLE.md#14-monetization--the-ethical-revenue-model)

Restated constraint (Principle 1): **sell time, depth, convenience, identity, and
access — never power, advantage, or entry.** Within that, five durable revenue
lines, ordered by fit-to-market.

## 1. Rewarded video advertising (primary early revenue)

With no paid IAP, ads carry the ~96% who never pay (the Wordscapes model).
- **Rewarded only**, opt-in, specific reward ("Watch for 50 coins" lifts opt-in
  10–20 pts). Minimize interstitials; no forced ads that break the 3–5 min loop.
- **Hybrid mediation across 5+ networks** (AdMob + Meta + Unity + ironSource/
  LevelPlay + AppLovin MAX) lifts eCPM 40–100% and fixes SA's patchy fill.
- **Plan conservatively:** SA rewarded eCPM ~$1–5; blended ARPDAU well below the
  ~$0.08 global casual average. **Volume, not per-user value.**
- **Meter the faucet** so ads fund the business without wrecking the economy
  ([`ECONOMY.md`](./ECONOMY.md)). **Status: not started (Phase 2).**

## 2. Subscription — "MzansiWord Plus" (primary long-term)

Gates **depth and convenience, never power** (chess.com/NYT playbook): no ads,
unlimited practice/archive, post-game analysis, extra streak shields (convenience,
not advantage), exclusive cosmetics, offline packs.
- **Explicitly NOT gated:** the daily prize puzzle (identical and free for all —
  fairness + CPA) and anything improving win chance. **A subscriber and a free
  player have exactly equal odds in every prize event.** This is the line that
  keeps us legal and trusted.
- Price for SA reality — small monthly, annual discount, family plans. Conversion
  will be low (~1–1.5%, SA card penetration lower still); a long-tail line.

## 3. Cosmetics (the identity economy)

Directly sellable *because they confer zero advantage* (Fortnite). SA-cultural
themes, avatars, frames, seasonal collections, local-artist collabs. Grows into a
major line over five years ([`PROGRESSION.md`](./PROGRESSION.md)).

## 4. Sponsor & branded content (the scale revenue)

Branded tournaments, sponsored puzzle packs, telco zero-rating, prize-pool
underwriting. Biggest ceiling; path to national-institution status. See
[`SPONSORS.md`](./SPONSORS.md).

## 5. Education & B2B licensing (the durable revenue)

Paid teacher/admin analytics tiers, district/provincial licences, CSR-funded free
tiers, corporate team-building tournaments. Slow to start, very sticky. See
[`EDUCATION.md`](./EDUCATION.md).

## Why this is a defense, not just a plan

No single line depends on selling power, so none threatens the legal foundation.
A game that can't sell whales must build many honest streams — and honest streams
are more durable. **Ads fund early ops; sponsors/telco fund prizes and scale;
subs/cosmetics monetize the core ethically; education/B2B is the durable base.**

## KPIs

ARPDAU (blended), rewarded-ad opt-in %, eCPM by network, sub conversion & churn,
cosmetic attach rate, sponsor revenue and renewal.
